Detailed Methodology

Method

  • For each game, a row is added to the matrix
  • For each game n such that i beats j, matrix[n][i]++, matrix[n][j]--, results_vector[n] = log(ratconst)
  • Postseason games are weighted more heavily using a linear multiplier of 1/6
  • Margin-of-victory is accounted for using a hyperbolic tangent function, capping at 1/6
  • The system is solved using least squares; 1 is added to the diagonal to insure non-singulartiy, e^x for every parameter

Premises

  • Each team's rating represents a context-aware average of their opponents' ratings
  • Game outcomes can be weighted by factors such as blowout margin and postseason status
  • Regularization helps stabilize rankings in sparse or disconnected schedules
  • Without any modifiers, we assume, without correction, that a team who beats another will be 5/3 as good as the team they beat
  • The final ratings are scaled such that the average team is scored at approximately 1
