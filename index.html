Alex Wang's Computer Sports Rankings

This site houses my MPCA algorithm ratings. MPCA (Matrix-PCA) uses PCA seasonally to determine weightings for home-field advantage and margin of victory, and a Laplacian matrix is used, along with these gamescores, in order to adjust for strength of schedule and produce an unbiased rating based on what we value in the real world.
